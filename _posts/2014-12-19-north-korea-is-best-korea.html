On Thursday  I saw a photo on Reddit. A theme of many photos I find myself browsing - Kim Jong-Un on one of his many tours of North Korean workplaces. The caption on the Reddit post was pretty funny. I, of course, immediately reposted it on Twitter and spent 2 minutes daydreaming up my own caption.

24 hours later it's my most popular tweet of all time.

https://twitter.com/jonginn/status/545536088312672256

Within an hour, it had 400 retweets. My TweetDeck became unusable from the weight of Favouriting, RTing and replies. It was phenomenal. At the time of writing (26 hours later), this tweet has 2,128 Retweets and 1,045 favourites.

I didn't expect this to happen. Looking at it, it has all the ingredients to do well at that point in time. The content, the whimsical scenario, and being swiftly RT'd by big users on Twitter (@iamdevloper being key) meant it spread incredibly quickly.
